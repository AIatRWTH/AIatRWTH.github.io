ABOUT US

We are a student club dedicated to the study of artificial intelligence and machine learning. Our goal is to provide a supportive community where members can learn and discuss the latest developments in AI, regardless of their background. Through talks and events, we aim to keep our members up-to-date with the latest trends and technologies in AI. Join and explore with us the world of AI!

The beginning of every project lies in the idea. Dealing with the problem and team building are of great importance here. A strong team is the key to success and promotes the exchange of knowledge and skills. In the AI Club, we create an atmosphere of collaborative learning and discovery to expand our knowledge of artificial intelligence and develop innovative solutions.

Debate

Debating is crucial for those interested in AI. We deepen our understanding through discussion and analysis of different perspectives. Debating develops critical thinking and communication skills. We foster an open and respectful environment that encourages members to improve their debating skills

Projects

Project building is a great way to gain practical AI experience and apply theoretical knowledge into practice. The AI Club promotes project building and supports members in their implementation. Our goal is to create an environment where everyone can develop innovative solutions.
